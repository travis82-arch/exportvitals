Wearable Export Viewer

A calm, local-first way to view and explore your wearable export ZIP files in the browser.

When you import an Oura export ZIP, parsing and metric generation run in your browser. Imported files and derived health data are stored locally on your device and are not sent to app server endpoints.

FAQ

Can I export my Oura data?

Yes. Export from your Oura account, then open the ZIP here.

How can I view my Oura export locally?

Open the dashboard and import your ZIP directly in-browser.

Does this upload my files to a server?

No. Import and metric generation happen locally on your device.

Is this affiliated with Oura?

No. This is an independent, unofficial open-source project.

Do I need an account to use it?

No account is required to use the viewer.

Is Fitbit support planned?

Yes, Fitbit export support is planned for a future release.

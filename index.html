FAQs

Does ExportVitals upload my Oura ZIP?

No. ExportVitals is designed around a local-first workflow. Your supported export file is parsed in your browser rather than uploaded to an ExportVitals server for parsing.

Can ExportVitals sync directly with my Oura Ring?

No. ExportVitals does not pair with the ring, connect over Bluetooth, or sync new ring data. It works only after you have downloaded an export file through Oura’s official tools.

Can ExportVitals help if my phone is too old for the Oura app?

Partly. ExportVitals cannot install the Oura app on an unsupported phone or replace the mobile app for syncing. If you can sync with another supported phone and download your export, ExportVitals can help you review that exported data locally.

Can ExportVitals replace Oura on the Web?

No. Oura on the Web is an official Oura account service. ExportVitals is an unofficial local viewer for downloaded export files.

What should I do first: sync, export, or import into ExportVitals?

Use this order: sync your ring with the official Oura app, export your data through Oura’s official tools, open ExportVitals, import the supported ZIP locally, and review your charts in the browser.

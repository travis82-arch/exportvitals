How to export Oura data

If you are looking for how to export Oura data, use the official Oura account/export area to download your Oura export ZIP.

Exact export steps may change over time, so always follow the latest guidance in official Oura account tools.

After download, open ExportVitals and import Oura export ZIP to review your data locally in the browser.

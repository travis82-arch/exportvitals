Wearable export viewer with Oura ZIP support

Many people can download wearable exports but still need a readable dashboard. Raw files such as CSV tables inside an Oura export ZIP can be difficult to review day by day. ExportVitals helps bridge that gap by turning supported export files into a local browser dashboard that is easier to scan.

Today, ExportVitals supports Oura export ZIP files. If you want an Oura export ZIP viewer to view Oura export in browser and analyze Oura export locally, this page explains what is available now and what is still planned.

ExportVitals is a local-first wearable export viewer. Parsing and metric generation run in your browser, no account is required for ExportVitals, and no upload flow is required to use the dashboard. The broader direction is support for wearable exports in general, not only one vendor format.

What this page solves

Downloading your export is only the first step. Most exports are structured for portability, not readability. ExportVitals reads supported files and renders trend views so you can inspect changes over time without building spreadsheets manually.

Current support and planned support

  • Live now: Oura export ZIP support.
  • Planned: Fitbit and other export formats.
  • Important: planned support is not live yet.

Export availability and the exact content in your export depend on official account/export tools. If the source export does not include a field, ExportVitals cannot infer it. Some official-app metrics may rely on proprietary algorithms or data streams that are not present in export files, so dashboard values may not exactly match every in-app card.

Who this is for

This is useful for people who want portable, readable views of personal wearable data, including quantified-self users and privacy-conscious users who prefer local analysis.

Limits and expectations

ExportVitals is unofficial, not affiliated with Oura, and not a medical diagnostic tool. It is designed for personal data inspection, trend awareness, and local file-based review.

FAQ

Does ExportVitals upload my health data?

No. The workflow is local-first in your browser.

Do I need an ExportVitals account?

No account is required to import supported exports and view charts.

Is ExportVitals affiliated with Oura?

No. It is unofficial and not affiliated with Oura.

Does ExportVitals support Fitbit right now?

No. Fitbit support is planned but not yet implemented.

Why might some values be missing?

The dashboard can only show what exists in the export package and known format.
